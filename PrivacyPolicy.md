# Privacy Policy 🔒

Your privacy matters. This document explains how the GB Utils App handles your data.

---

## 1. No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.  
We do not track users or gather analytics.

---

## 2. Local Storage Only

All your data (tasks and expenses) are stored locally on your device using secure storage methods such as AsyncStorage.

---

## 3. No Internet Usage

This app works completely offline.  
It does not require an internet connection and does not send any data to external servers.

---

## 4. Data Control

You have full control over your data.  
Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored information.

---

## 5. Security

Since all data remains on your device, it is private and not accessible to third parties.

---

## Summary

GB Utils App is a fully offline application.  
Your information stays on your device and is never collected, shared, or transmitted anywhere.
